It's Donuts for Everyone

Make a little or a lot with Wilton donut pans


Weekend mornings were made for donuts. So make and bake your own using one of our 6, 12 or 20-cavity pans. From regular size to mini, you'll have plenty to serve for breakfast, Sunday brunch or an afternoon snack. The pans have a non-stick surface, so your donuts will slide right out after baking and cleanup is easy. Once your donuts have cooled, frost them with Wilton Doughnut Stand Dessert Glaze in Vanilla, Chocolate or Strawberry and top with rainbow jimmies. Or use a basic vanilla glaze recipe and tint in different colors to match a party theme. Do-nut limit the possibilities!

How Do You Donut?

Tennis Ball Donuts

It's game, set, match for donut lovers with these tennis-inspired treats. Bake your cake donuts using the non-stick 6-cavity pan and let cool. Whip up a batch of confectioners' sugar glaze, reserving about 1/4 cup. Tint the rest using Lemon Yellow and a touch of Leaf Green icing colors, then dip the tops of your donuts into the glaze. Let stand for about 30 minutes. Prepare a decorating bag with tip 4, fill with the reserved white glaze and pipe wavy lines on top of the yellow to resemble a tennis ball. Be sure to make enough to serve up a single or a double, depending on how hungry everyone is. They'll be the smash of any breakfast table.

Birthday Cake Donut Tower

There's nothing better than cake for your birthday, except maybe a tower of glazed donuts with sprinkles on top. And it's so simple to put together. Bake cake donuts using the non-stick 12-cavity pan and then cool. Top them with your favorite flavor of glaze and sprinkle on rainbow jimmies. Let them set for about a half hour, insert some candles and there you have it, a birthday cake of donuts! Just blow out the candles, grab a donut and go!

Chocolate, Vanilla and Strawberry Glazed Donuts

Who doesn't love a glazed donut? It's just about everyone's favorite breakfast food. Make a big batch for Sunday brunch using our regular or mini size non-stick donut pans. Once baked, ice them with Wilton Doughnut Stand Dessert Glaze in chocolate, vanilla or strawberry, sold separately. You can even layer the glazes and create cool designs on top. Finish with a few chocolate or rainbow jimmies and you're ready to indulge in these yummy treats.
