This comfy sofa chair is the perfect accessory to help teach your baby how to sit.

Features:

  • Upright seating position - helps stabilize your infant's back, while they learn to sit up.

  • Non-slip fabric - Prevents your infant from sliding onto the floor.

  • Lightweight and portable - easily provides your baby with a soft place to sit in any room of the house.

  • 360 degrees of comfy support - safely wraps your baby in plush comfort as she observes her surroundings.

  • Size: 43cm x 43cm x 20cm / 17.7" x 15.7" x 7.8"
